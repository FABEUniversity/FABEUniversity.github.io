Student Services of the Future

At FABE University, we recognize that the use of multiple different tools for administrative tasks, as seen at universities like TU Dresden, creates unnecessary complexity for students, faculty, and administrators. Therefore, we want to establish a unified platform called the FABE Online Learning and Teaching Platform (FOLT) to streamline all administrative activities.
Through FOLT, students are able to handle everything from admission, over course registration, to participating in lectures, engaging with your mates in project work and exam registration as well as grading in one platform! Available everywhere and always - Education as a Service!

AI Teaching and Learning Experience (AITLE)

Your AI Buddy

At FABE University, we understand the daily challenges of balancing academic responsibilities and finding the right resources. That's why we've created the AI Teaching and Learning Experience (AITLE), your personal AI-powered study buddy. As an AI-first university, we're committed to enhancing your learning journey and academic success. AITLE offers personalized support to help you maximize your time and effort. Embrace the future of education with AITLE, and discover how effortless and rewarding your studies can be.

  • Personalized Course Content and Learning Paths

    At FABE University, professors and staff design courses using our comprehensive Course Design framework and guidelines. - Based on these designs, AITLE can automatically create lecture videos, presentation slides, and find you additional reference materials from our comprehensive online library! - Didn't understand a particular topic in the lecture? Need a specific concept explained again? AITLE has you covered with customized learning materials and improved study guides. This greatly enhances accessibility, reduces skill gaps, and helps students overcome language barriers to actively engage and participate in lectures. - AITLE also analyzes your academic progress and learning style to understand how and when you learn best, adapting your learning experience by recommending personalized learning paths. Whether you need extra help in a specific subject or want to accelerate your learning, AITLE tailors its recommendations to fit your unique needs.

  • Interactive Exercises and Feedback

    AITLE not only develops lectures, but also excercises based on the given course design. - AITLE supports you during your excercises by answering questions and giving hints and recommendations to solve the problems. - Through the automatic analysis of your responses and answers, it can not only give you feedback on how successfull you were, but AITLE also understands where your areas for improvement are and suggests further excercise material to you to help you master difficult concepts and boost your academic performance.

  • Personalized Course Planning and Management

    Struggling to organize your study schedule? AITLE is here to assist you with optimal course planning and management, suggesting the best times to study each subject and when to review material for improved retention. It helps you structure your semester plan, ensuring you stay ahead in your coursework. - AITLE aids students in creating and managing their course schedules, integrating seamlessly with the university’s registration system. - Utilizing academic history, preferences, and degree requirements, AITLE suggests optimal timetables tailored to each student. - In case of scheduling conflicts, AITLE offers alternative course options that align with the student’s needs and preferences. - For enhanced organization, AITLE syncs with digital calendars like Google Calendar or Outlook, ensuring students never miss a class, assignment, or important university event.

  • Administrative Support

    In the bustling world of academia, it's easy to feel overwhelmed by the flood of questions and administrative hurdles. At FABE University, we understand that finding answers to specific questions often requires a prolonged search through university websites, uploaded documents, and shared files, resulting in wasted time that could otherwise be used for valuable work. "Until when do we have to enroll in this course? How can I access the university's online library?" These are just some of the questions every student has likely asked themselves.
    - For all of these questions you can now use AITLE! Just ask your question and you will receive not only an answer but also links to relevant sources for verification.
     - This approach ensures that all university stakeholders can find information more easily, stay informed about the latest changes, and focus their energy on contributing to the university's value. Additionally, it reduces the workload of administrative staff, as they will have to answer fewer inquiries.

  • Academic Advising & Career Guidance

    AITLE offers comprehensive academic advising and career guidance to support your journey at FABE University. - Career Services: Receive internship and job alerts tailored to your career aspirations and academic background, helping you stay informed about relevant opportunities. - Resume and Cover Letter Assistance: AITLE provides tips, templates, and AI-driven reviews to enhance your resumes and cover letters, ensuring they stand out to potential employers. - Academic Advising: Track your degree progress with AITLE, which monitors completed credits, GPA, and remaining requirements. It offers advising recommendations, suggesting courses, minors, or electives aligned with your academic performance and career goals. - Document Management: Easily manage academic documents such as transcripts, advising notes, and degree plans through AITLE’s centralized platform.

Making online Classes a Reality

At FABE University, our commitment to realize hybrid learning meaning a combination of online and offline learning and teaching is evident through the integration of cutting-edge tools designed to facilitate seamless communication and collaboration among students and faculty. Utilizing messaging and collaboration platforms, interactive whiteboards, and robust live streaming technologies, we ensure that our students receive a dynamic educational experience. These innovations not only foster greater engagement and interaction but also provide flexibility for students to access lectures and participate in discussions from anywhere. By embracing these technologies, we are not just adapting to current challenges but also shaping the future of education with enriched, interactive learning environments at FABE University.
